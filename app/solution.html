Solution
Our company proposes WEMAX 1.0 Energy Technology Solution for Telecom industry for two grid connections set-up:
  • bad-grid connection;
  • off-grid connection.

BAD-GRID

In many developing parts of the world connection to the grid network is not stable during the 24 hours . In fact, power grid connection can be interrupted due to blackouts, outages, unexpected power switch offs and other reasons.

In order to compensate for the power interruptions, in case of such problems mobile network operators (MNO), tower management companies (TOWCO) and energy service companies (ESCO) utilize diesel generators, solar panels and wind turbines as partial solution.

- Power generation capacity of wind turbines is restricted. Wind turbines can only be constructed in places with sufficient wind flow speed and continuous presence of wind flow

-Solar panels are also limited with regards to power generation capacity subject to number of sunny hours and days during a year

- Diesel generators can not be solution from the ecological point of view. In addition, they can only supply power for a limited number of hours and require substantial CapEx and OpEx investments.

WEMAX 1.0 Energy Technology Solution provides possibility to address the above-mentioned power interruptions by improving performance of batteries

Off-GRID

In order to provide more people with high quality communication service and taking into account emergence of latest technologies such as 5G, Internet of Things (IoT), MNOs are building more towers in remote locations without connection to the centralized grid network.

For most potential regions with low electricity access , as of 2014 the number of mobile towers with no connection to the grid comprised 320 100, which makes 31 % of all towers. This figure increased up to 389 600 towers in 2020, which makes 33 % of total number of towers.

For off-grid towers the only power source can be batteries, wind turbines, solar panels and diesel generators.

WEMAX 1.0 Energy Technology Solution provides possibility to address the above-mentioned power interruptions by providing mixed solution utilizing solar panels and batteries (with improved performance).
WEMAX 1.0 can provide uninterrupted power supply for locations without connection to the grid.
